Meet MC Farland.

As the self-proclaimed National President of the American Band Dad Association, I'm dedicated to supporting band programs by being present at performances, aiding with instruments and events, and fostering an inclusive community. American Band Dads commit to supporting all band members, directors, and parents, ensuring a thriving environment where every member feels valued and inspired to reach their musical aspirations.
